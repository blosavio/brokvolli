brokvolli.stateful-transducers-kv

Statefull, ‘kv-ified’ transducers. Not recommended for use with multi-threaded brokvolli.multi/transduce-kv.

Safe to use with single-threaded brokvolli.single/transduce-kv.

See also brokvolli.transducers-kv.

dedupe-kv

(dedupe-kv)

Returns a stateful dedupe-ing transducer like clojure.core/dedupe, but with an additional arity-3 of result, keydex, and value.
